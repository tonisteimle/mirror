Übung

Erstelle eine animierte Feature-Card mit Hover-Effekt und Icon-Animation:

Lösung 
// Feature Card mit Animation
Frame w 180, bg #1a1a1a, rad 12, pad 20, gap 12, cursor pointer, anim fade-in
  Frame w 48, h 48, bg #2563eb, rad 12, center
    Icon "rocket", ic white, is 24
  Text "Schnellstart", col white, weight 500
  Text "In Sekunden einsatzbereit.", col #888, fs 13
  hover:
    scale 1.02
    bg #252525
    Frame w 48, h 48, bg #1d4ed8, rad 12, center
      Icon "rocket", ic white, is 24, anim bounce

// Mehrere Cards
Frame hor, gap 12
  Frame w 140, bg #1a1a1a, rad 12, pad 16, gap 10, cursor pointer, anim slide-in
    Frame w 40, h 40, bg #10b981, rad 10, center
      Icon "check", ic white, is 20
    Text "Einfach", col white, weight 500, fs 14
    Text "Intuitive UI", col #888, fs 12
    hover:
      scale 1.03
      shadow lg

  Frame w 140, bg #1a1a1a, rad 12, pad 16, gap 10, cursor pointer, anim slide-in
    Frame w 40, h 40, bg #f59e0b, rad 10, center
      Icon "zap", ic white, is 20
    Text "Schnell", col white, weight 500, fs 14
    Text "Blitzschnell", col #888, fs 12
    hover:
      scale 1.03
      shadow lg

Zusammenfassung

  • anim preset – fade-in, slide-in, scale-in, bounce, pulse, shake, spin
  • hover: scale, opacity – Hover-Transitions
  • enter: anim – Animation beim Erscheinen
  • exit: anim – Animation beim Verschwinden
  • Kombinierbar für komplexe Effekte
